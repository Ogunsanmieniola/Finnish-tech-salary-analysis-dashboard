This project analyzes salary trends in the Finnish tech industry using SQL and Power BI. The goal is to explore how different factors such as job role, years of experience, work type, company type, and age group influence salary levels.

Tools Used

SQL (MySQL) – Data querying and analysis

Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation

Excel – Data preparation

Key Questions Explored

What is the average salary by job role?

How does salary change with years of experience?

What are the top 5 highest paying roles?

What are the lowest paying roles?

Do remote workers earn more than hybrid workers?

Which company type pays the highest salary?

Which age group earns the most?

Key Insights

Salaries generally increase with years of experience.

The highest salary peak occurs around 26 years of experience.

Some roles such as Architect and Technical Director appear among the highest paying.

Remote workers earn slightly higher salaries than hybrid workers.

Consulting firms tend to offer higher salaries than non-profit organizations.

Dashboard

The Power BI dashboard provides an interactive view of salary trends including:
Average salary by role
Salary distribution by experience
Top 5 highest paying roles
Salary comparison by company type
Salary comparison by work type
Salary by age group
