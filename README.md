This project analyzes salary trends in the Finnish tech industry based on experience, job role, work type, company type, and age group. Key Questions asked;
What roles have the highest salaries?
How does experience impact salary?
Do remote workers earn more than hybrid workers?
Which age groups earn the highest salaries?
KEY INSIGHT 
Salaries generally increase with years of experience.
The highest salary peak occurs around 26 years of experience.
Architect and Technical Director roles are among the highest paying positions.
Remote workers earn slightly higher salaries than hybrid workers.
Consulting firms offer higher salaries than non-profit organizations.
